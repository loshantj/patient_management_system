About Our Hospital

The National Institute of Infectious Diseases (NIID), formerly known as the Infectious Diseases Hospital (IDH), is the focal point, main referral center, and training center for infectious diseases in Sri Lanka. It is situated in Angoda, Colombo on a 34-acre block of land, consisting of 300 beds.
